About

I am a Postodoctoral researcher at TRIUMF specializing on nuclear theory and the quantum many body problem. My work focuses on clustering and pairing effects in nuclear systems and the nature of the nuclear force. With these systems ranging from atomic nuclei to neutron-star matter, these effects generate fascinating physics at many scales and a plethora of experimental/observational signatures.

Below you can find more information about me and the projects I am working on as well as links to my publications and CV.
